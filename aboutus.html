About Us

Through our website, customers can purchase tours to places without established park systems. Since they do not have a nationwide park infrastructure, it provides our company with an avenue to show those less explored regions of the world. Tour guides are exceptionally trained and are willing to assist and answer questions.

By enjoying yourself on our tours, you will be contributing to a cleaner environment! Our company focuses on changing the world by giving 25 percent of all profit made from trips to help local communities deal with the harmful effects of pollution. We donate to organizations The Great Global Cleanup, Clean Up The World, and Ocean Conservancy to assist us in our efforts to clean less fortunate areas of the world.

Our tours are better than others because we provide trips to remote destinations. Unlike the Arches or Grand Canyon, our areas are uncrowded and give the same breathtaking views. Our trail maintenance crew will be checking on the paths regularly, clearing them of any hazards.
